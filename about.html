About

Our Story

El Paraíso Restaurant serves the best authentic Central American Food in Cockeysville/Lutherville Timonium.

Founded in 2015, we are a small restaurant and have embraced an intimate setting. Here you will enjoy a comfortable experience, grounded in our love of good times with genuine friends and most of all: great food!

We are not your standard Tex-Mex style restaurant. We serve Central American/Salvadoran food—different but delicious. We are known for our awesome pupusas and tacos. Please take a look at our full menu to see what we offer.

Our Location

Our restaurant is part of the adjoining grocery store, La Favorita, located in the convenient Cranbrook Shopping Center in Cockeysville, right off of York Road.
